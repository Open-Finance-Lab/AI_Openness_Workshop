## Past Workshops
*  **AAAI 25: Workshop on Open-Source AI for Mainstream Use** (By Peter Santhanam, Alexandru Cioba, et al.) [Link](https://the-ai-alliance.github.io/AAAI-25-Workshop-on-Open-Source-AI-for-Mainstream-Use/)
